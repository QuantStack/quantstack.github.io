## Andreas Trawöger {#identifier heading2}

### DevOps Engineer {#identifier heading3}

 
 Andreas Trawöger is a DevOps engineer at QuantStack. He previously worked as a sysadmin for multiple Internet service provider and a startup for test automation before getting involved with Data Science through his interest in Open Data and public access to earth observation data.
 

Currently he is working on scaling and performance improvements for <a target="_blank" href="https://github.com/mamba-org/quetz">Quetz</a> and making the <a target="_blank" href="https://jupyter.org">Jupyter ecosystem</a> more accessible for everyone.

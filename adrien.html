Adrien Delsalle
Scientific software developer

@adriendelsalle

Adrien Delsalle is a Scientific Software Developer at QuantStack. He used to work in the aeronautical industry on turbofan engines modeling and studied at the French aerospace ISAE-ENSMA engineering school.

Adrien co-founded CoSApp when working at Safran, a modeling framework relying on the Python scientific stack and JupyterLab.

As an open source developer, Adrien contributes to xtensor and quetz. He is also active in the Jupyter andOpenCascade ecosystems.

David Brochart
Scientific software developer

@davidbrochart

David Brochart is a Scientific Software Developer at QuantStack. David used to work in the semiconductor industry as a digital circuit designer, mostly using FPGAs. He studied telecommunications at the French "Telecom Bretagne" engineering school, with major in digital circuit design.

David also worked as a researcher in hydrology at Irstea, a French scientific research institute, where he heavily used the Python scientific stack.

As an open source developer, David has been involved in several GIS projects, including Pangeo and IPyLeaflet. He is now a core contributor of a number of Jupyter projects, including jupyter-server, jupyter-client and nbclient.

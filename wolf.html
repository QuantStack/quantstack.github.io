Wolf Vollprecht
Robotics software developer

@wuoulf | @wolfv

Wolf is currently pursuing his master in Robotics, Systems and Controls at ETH Zurich. But he's always had a knack for Open Source. while working on JET, a framework to compile NumPy, he was really missing a library like xtensor.

And when Wolf saw the powers that wizards of template metaprogramming hold, he wanted to learn it too. What better way than to contribute to xtensor !

He hopes to install xtensor on some of his robots. For example, the BeachBot, developed with Disney Research in Zurich, certainly could draw much better with some fast linear algebra.

ABOUT US

Our clients range from research laboratories, financial institutions, software corporations, and government agencies.

We are a team of expert open-source developers, the very same people behind a number of technologies that you already use...

We can provide a variety of services for your organization:
  • Devising and producing high-performance scientific computing software following the current best practices, and making use of the latest available technologies.
  • Development of extensions and features for Jupyter and JupyterLab.
  • Help improve your use of the open-source scientific stack in your organization through custom development, integration and in-person training.
